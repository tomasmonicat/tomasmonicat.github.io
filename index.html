Analyzing my Chess.com games

Retrieving, cleaning and processing data from my chess.com games. Analysis incoming!

I am an amateur chess player and a data lover. This project is the combination of both, where I used the chess.com API to retrieve information of my games. I clean it and process it in python. The script used here can be used to retreive information of your own games as well, just with your username id, year and month you would like to get. I am working on the analysis, will upload as soon as it is ready!

Visualization of temperature variations

Analysis and visualization of temperature variations near Buenos Aires over 40 years

The NCEI holds daily records of over 122,000 metheorological stations over the world. I processed the data from the stations near the city of Buenos Aires, Argentina, where I am from. After the cleaning and processing, the minimum and maximum temperatures across the 1973-1983 decade where plotted and compared to the ones across the 2003-2013 decade.

Analysis of North American sports leagues

Data cleaning and statistical analysis of performance of teams from the Big 4

In this project I scraped data about the 2021-2022 seasons onf the NFL, MLB, NBA and NHL leagues and information about the metropolitan areas where each team is based. The data was cleaned and processed to ultimately try to find patterns and relationships.
